About ProNet

  • The ProNet platform strives to deliver a simplistic networking platform that allows users to engage with colleagues or future business partners. Having a dashboard to share personal experiences/goals, a networking tab that allows for individuals to view one another’s profiles, explore page that projects similar interest posts by the use of tags, and a business page that allows for firms to share opportunities/events; are a few of the many possibilities we hope to achieve when tackling this project. The overarching goal is to allow individuals to explore and connect through common interest and ambition in the ease of just clicking a button.
  • ProNet aims to deliver a simple, professional networking platform that focuses more on the relationship/interactions of the business nature and environment, rather than personal communication between friends and family. With the implementation of the various technical requirements (HTML, JavaScript, etc.), our team will create a website for business leaders and potential business employees to utilize for networking purposes in the business world. The project will offer multiple benefits to its users, allowing them to interact with other organizations and colleagues, and even promote their own work. To create the website, we will implement elements of the project in phases, starting with the basic structure of the site and building up to key features, such as a networking tab. Our final product will introduce the ProNet website, offering users a business networking platform to share, promote, and connect to other businesses and business users around the world.
